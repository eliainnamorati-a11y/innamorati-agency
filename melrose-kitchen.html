Our Project

Our project with Melrose Kitchen focused on developing a refreshed, modern brand identity that aligns with their mission of creating happiness through delicious, healthy food. By designing a clean and modern visual identity, along with thoughtful content and packaging, we helped Melrose Kitchen present a cohesive and contemporary brand that reflects their values and appeals to their growing customer base.
